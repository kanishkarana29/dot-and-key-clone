Dot and key is a skincare brand that offers a range of products that are made with botanicals and actives. Some of their products include: Sunscreens, Moisturizers, Serums, and Facewash.

Anisha Agarwal Saraf and Suyash Saraf are the main brain behind the idea of 'Dot & Key'.

Anisha Agarwal Saraf

Co-founder who connected the dots of skincare to find the key to flawless beauty. She struggled with balancing work and family, but says "it was all worth it".

Suyash Saraf

Co-founder who has a Bachelor's degree in Real Estate Development and Entrepreneurship/Entrepreneurial Studies from Indiana University Bloomington. He also attended Cass Business School in 2012-2013.
The couple bootstrapped the brand with an initial investment of around Rs 3 crore after eight months of trying and testing.
Here are some things to know about Dot & Key:
  • Clean
    • Dot & Key products are free of Parabens, Sulfates, Essential oils, Mineral oils, GMOs and Phthalates.
  • Dermatologically tested
    • Dot & Key products are dermatologist-tested and suitable for all skin types.
  • Cruelty-free
    • Dot & Key products are certified cruelty free i.e.-the products are not tested on poor animals before their launches.
  • Product Range
    • Dot & Key products include sunscreens, moisturizers, lip balms/masks, toners, face washes and many more.We also offer exciting deals for our customers.
  • Brand Name
    • The name Dot & Key was chosen to represent the idea of providing "keys" to missing "dots" in people's skincare routines.
  • Expansion
    • Dot & Key recently expanded into nutraceuticals under the brand IKWI.Many celebrities use our products and we have shipped through all across India.
